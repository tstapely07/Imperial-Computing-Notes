For a [[Matrices|matrix]] to be in **REF**:
* The [[Pivot|pivot]] of each row must be strictly to the **right** of the **pivots** of the rows **above**.
* Any rows with zero entries must be at the bottom.

>[!Example]
>Here is a **matrix** in **REF**:
>$$\begin{bmatrix} 
> 2 & 4 & 5 & 0 \\
> 0 & 0 & 10 & 2 \\
> 0 & 0 & 0 & 0
>\end{bmatrix}
>$$
