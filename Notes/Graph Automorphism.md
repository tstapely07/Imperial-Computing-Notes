> [!Abstract] Definition
> An **automorphism** on a [[Graphs|graph]] $G$ is an [[Graph Isomorphism|isomorphism]] from $G$ to itself.

For example, this graph has $8$ **automorphisms**:
* ![[Pasted image 20260116162953.png|150]]
>[!Info]
>$1$ can map to 4 different places.
>If we fix $1$, then $2$ can map to 2 places.
>If we fix $2$, then $3$ can map to 1 place.
>$4$ is now also fixed.
>Total = $4\times 2\times 1 \times 1 = 8$ (including the identity).
