> [!Abstract] Definition
> [[Edge-Triggered D-Type Flip-Flop|Edge-triggered]] control inputs are known as **clocks**.
> * A **clock** is a continuous oscillating signal.
> * ![[Pasted image 20260406143556.png|471]]
> 

**Clocks** are used to synchronise a sequence of operations that occur at regular intervals.
