Informally, a problem is considered **tractable** if it can be computed in a reasonable amount of [[Orders of Complexity|time]].
* We will focus on the [[Worst Case vs Average Case Analysis|worst case complexity]], $W(n)$.

> [!Thesis]
> A **problem** is **tractable** iff it can be computed within **polynomially** many steps in the **worst case**:
> * $W(n)\le p(n)$ for some **polynomial** $p(n)$.
> * We call **polynomially** **p-time** for short.
