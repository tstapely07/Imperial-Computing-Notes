The **standard** [[ordered basis]] is the "simplest" set of [[Vectors|vectors]] that describe a [[Vector Spaces|vector space]].

For $\mathbb{R}^n$, the standard basis would be:
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
0 \\
0 \\
\dots \\
0
\end{bmatrix},
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
1 \\
0 \\
\dots \\
0
\end{bmatrix},\dots,
\begin{bmatrix}
0 \\
0 \\
\dots \\
0 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}
$$
