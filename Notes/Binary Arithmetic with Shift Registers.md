[[Shift registers]] can perform [[Signed Binary Arithmetic|binary arithmetic]] operations by [[Binary|bit]] shifting.
* Multiplying by $2$ can be achieved by shifting the **bits** one place to the left, and filling the bottom bit with a $0$.
* Dividing by $2$ can be achieved by shifting the **bits** one place to the right, and discarding the bottom bit.
