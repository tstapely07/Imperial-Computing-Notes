From [[Armstrong's axioms]], we can derive other rules about [[functional dependencies]].

The **union rule** states that:
$$X\to Y,X\to Z\equiv X\to YZ$$

The **pseudotransitivity rule** states that:
$$X\to Y, WY\to Z\models WX\to Z$$

The **decomposition rule** states that:
$$X\to Y,Z\subseteq Y\models X\to Z$$
