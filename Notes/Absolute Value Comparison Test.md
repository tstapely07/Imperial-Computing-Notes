The [[comparison test]] can be extended to check for the **absolute convergence** of a [[Series|series]].

> [!Theorem]
> Let $\sum_{n=1}^\infty b_n$ be a **convergent series** with **non-negative** **terms**.
> If $|a_n|\le b_n$ for all $n\in\mathbb{N}^+$, then the **series** $\sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n$ [[Absolute Convergence|converges absolutely]], and therefore **converges**.
>
