> [!Abstract] Definition
> A **decision problem** is one with a strict yes/no answer, such as determining if a [[Graphs|graph]] has a [[Euler Paths and Circuits|Euler]] [[Paths|path]] or [[Hamiltonian Paths and Circuits|Hamiltonian path]].
> * Sorting a list is **not** a **decision problem**.

A **decision problem** is **decided** by an algorithm $A$ if, for any input $x$, $A$ returns "yes" or "no", depending on whether $D(x)$.
* $A$ must always **terminate**.
