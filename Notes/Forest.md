> [!Abstract] Definition
> A **forest** is an [[Graphs|graph]] that contains **no** [[Cycles|cycles]].

This is equivalent to a **forest** being a **disjoint union** of [[Trees - Graphs|trees]].
If it consists of only one **component**, then it is just a **single tree**.
