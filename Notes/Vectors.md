**Vectors** are an **ordered collection** of **real numbers** arranged **vertically** as columns.
Geometrically, they represent some point in Euclidean space, ($p\in\mathbb{R}^n$).

>[!Example]
>$$\overrightarrow{b} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \\ 7 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}$$
