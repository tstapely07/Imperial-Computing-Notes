> [!Abstract] Definition
> The **image** of a [[Linear Mapping|linear mapping]] is its **range** in the **codomain**. It is a [[Vector Subspaces|subspace]] of the **codomain** [[Vector Spaces|vector space]].

For a [[Matrices|matrix]] $A$, the **image** is also called the **column space**.
It can be calculated as the [[Vector Span|span]] of the columns of $A$ that correspond to the [[Pivot|pivots]] in the [[Reduced Row Echelon Form (RREF)|RREF]].
$$\operatorname{Im}(A)=\operatorname{span}\{\text{pivot columns of }A\}$$

The [[Dimension|dimension]] of the **image** is the [[Matrix Rank|matrix rank]].
