The **rank** of a [[Matrices|matrix]] $M$ is the [[Dimension|dimension]] of the [[Image|image space]] -  $\operatorname{dim}(\operatorname{Im}(M))$
This is also equivalent to:
* The number of [[Linear Independence|linearly independent]] columns in $M$
* The number of [[Pivot|pivots]] when $M$ is in [[Reduced Row Echelon Form (RREF)|RREF]].

> [!Note]
> $\operatorname{rank}(M)=\operatorname{rank}(M^T)$

See also - [[rank nullity theorem]].
