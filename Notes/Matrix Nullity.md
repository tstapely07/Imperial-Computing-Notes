The **nullity** of a [[Matrices|matrix]] $M$ is the [[Dimension|dimension]] of the [[Kernel|kernel space]] - $\operatorname{dim}(\operatorname{Ker}(M))$
This is equivalent to:
* The number of **free variables** in the solution space of $M\overrightarrow{x}=\overrightarrow{0}$.
* The number of columns that **do not** have a [[Pivot|pivot]] when $M$ is in [[Reduced Row Echelon Form (RREF)|RREF]].

See also - [[rank nullity theorem]].
