For any [[Graphs|graph]] $G$ we can define a **relation** on $nodes(G)$ by:
* $x\sim y$ iff there is a path from $x$ to $y$
This can trivially be proved to be **reflexive**, **symmetric**, and **transitive**, making it an **equivalence relation**.

The **equivalence classes** of $\sim$ are the [[Nodes|nodes]] of the **connected components**.
