An **upper triangular matrix** is of the form:
$$A=\begin{bmatrix}a_1 & a & \dots & b \\
0 & a_2 & \dots & c \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots\ & \vdots \\
0 & 0 & \dots & d_n\end{bmatrix}
$$

A **lower triangular matrix** is of the form:
$$B = \begin{bmatrix} b_1 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ d & b_2 & \dots & 0 \\  \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ e & f & \dots & b_n \end{bmatrix}$$
