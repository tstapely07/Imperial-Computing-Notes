The **dimension** of a [[Vector Spaces|vector space]] or [[Vector Subspaces|subspace]] is the number of vectors in a [[Basis of a Subspace|basis]] for that **space**.

The **dimension** of an [[Affine Space|affine space]] is the dimension of the **parallel subspace**.
