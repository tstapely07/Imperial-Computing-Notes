> [!Abstract] Definition
> The **fan-out** of a [[Logic Gates|logic gate]] is the total number of subsequent **logic gate** inputs that its output connects to.
> 

Connecting too many **gates** can cause the voltage to drop too low, preventing $1$s from being registered.
* It can also increase the tine delay of the circuit.
