> [!Abstract] Definition
> A [[Decision Problems|decision problem]] $D$ is **NP-hard** if, for all problems $D'\in NP$, we have $D'\le D$.

Note that **NP-hard** problems are not necessarily in [[class NP]], they could be harder.
If $D$ is **NP-hard** and $D\le D'$ then $D'$ is also **NP-hard**, by the **transitivity** of [[Reduction|reduction]].
