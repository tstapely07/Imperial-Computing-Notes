The **triangle inequality** states that the **magnitude** of the **sum** of two [[Vectors|vectors]] is always less than or equal to the **sum** of their individual **magnitudes**:
$$|\vec{a}+\vec{b}|\le|\vec{a}|+|\vec{b}|$$

Just like the [[Cauchy-Schwarz inequality]], this is only equal if the two **vectors** are parallel, such that $\vec{a}=k\vec{b}$.
