When evaluating the area under a curve stretching to $\infty$, we cannot use a standard [[Partition (Calculus)|partition]]. instead, we use [[Limits|limits]].

For a function $f:[1,\infty)\to\mathbb{R}$ , the **improper integral** is defined as:
$$\lim_{c\to\infty}\int_1^cf(x)\;dx$$
* If the **limit** exists, and is a finite number, the [[Integration|integral]] **converges**.
* If the **limit** does not exist, or is $\pm\infty$, the **integral** **diverges**.
