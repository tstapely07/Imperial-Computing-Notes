> [!Abstract] Definition
> $A, B,\dots$ is the **set** of **attributes** of a **relation** $R(A,B,\dots)$.
> * This is often written without commas ($A,B\dots \\\equiv AB\dots$) 
> * We can refer to to a set of **attributes** as $\vec{X}$.
> * For some **relation** $R(A,B,\dots)$, $Attrs(R)$ yields $A,B,\dots$

> [!Abstract] Definition
> The **domain** of an **attribute** $Domain(A)$ is the **set** of **values** that the **attribute** can take.
> * For all $x$, $v_x^A\in Domain(A)$
