[[Binary]] **Addition** is performed column by column, carrying $1$s as necessary.
**Binary Subtraction** is performed column by column, with borrowing as necessary.
**Binary Multiplication** is performed just like decimal column multiplication.
**Binary Division** uses the formula $\frac{\text{dividend}}{\text{divisor}}=\text{quotient}+\frac{\text{remainder}}{\text{divisor}}$ and long division.

See also: [[Signed Binary Arithmetic]]
