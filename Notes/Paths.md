> [!Abstract] Definition
> A **path** in a [[Graphs|graph]] is a sequence of adjacent [[arcs|arcs]].

We usually describe **paths** by the [[Nodes|nodes]] passed through.
In this example [[Graphs|graph]], two paths include `1,2,3,4` and `2,5,2,3,3,4`:

> [!Abstract] Definition
> A **path** is **simple** if it has no repeated nodes.
