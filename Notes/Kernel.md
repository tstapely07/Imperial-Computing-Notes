> [!Abstract]  Definition
> The **kernel** of a [[Linear Mapping|linear mapping]] is the set of all input [[Vectors|vectors]] in the **domain** that are mapped to $\overrightarrow{0}$ in the **codomain**. it is a [[Vector Subspaces|subspace]] of the **domain** [[Vector Spaces|vector space]].

For a [[Matrices|matrix]] $A$, $\operatorname{Ker}(f_a)=\text{Set of all solutions of }A\overrightarrow{x}=\overrightarrow{0}$

The [[dimension]] of the **kernel** is the [[matrix nullity]].
