> [!Abstract] Definition
> The **linear combination** of $n$ [[Vectors|vectors]] $\{\overrightarrow{v_1},\overrightarrow{v_2},\dots,\overrightarrow{v_n}\}$ is of the form $\lambda_1\overrightarrow{v_1}+\lambda_2\overrightarrow{v_2}+\dots+\lambda_n\overrightarrow{v_n}\;,\;\forall i.\lambda_i\in\mathbb{R}$.
