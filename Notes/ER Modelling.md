> [!Abstract] Definition
> Before we can construct [[SQL Data Definition Language (DDL)|SQL]] tables, we must model the [[Universe of Discourse (UoD)|UoD]].
> This is known as **entity relationship modelling**.

We split the **UoD** into [[ER Entities|entities]] and [[ER Relationships|relationships]].
