While [[Karnaugh maps]] and [[Boolean algebra]] are useful for [[Circuit Minimisation|minimising]] small [[Logic Gates|logic]] circuits, they become much less feasible when dealing with larger real-world design problems.

Instead, we use **functional design**. 
* This approach uses modular building blocks such as [[multiplexers]], [[decoders]] and [[registers]] to design larger systems.
