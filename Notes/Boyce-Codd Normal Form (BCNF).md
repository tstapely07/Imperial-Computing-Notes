**Boyce-Codd normal form** is a **stricter version** of [[Third Normal Form (3NF)|3NF]].

> [!Abstract] Definition
> For every **non-trivial** [[Functional Dependencies|FD]], $X\to A$ on some [[Relations|relation]] $R$, $X$ must be a [[FDs and Keys|super-key]].
