A [[Basis of a Subspace|basis]] $\set{\vec{v_1},\vec{v_2},\dots,\vec{v_k}}$ is **orthogonal** if every [[Vectors|vector]] is **perpendicular** to every other **vector**.
* This means that their [[Inner Product|dot products]] are always zero:
$$\vec{v}_i \cdot \vec{v}_j = 0 \quad \forall i \neq j$$
