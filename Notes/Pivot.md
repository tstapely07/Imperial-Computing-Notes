The **pivot** of a row of a [[Matrices|matrix]] is its **leading coefficient** - the first **non-zero** entry in the row.

>[!Example]
>In this **row**, the **pivot** is $2$.
>$$\begin{bmatrix} 
> \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\
> 0 & 2 & 0 & 1 \\
> \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots
>\end{bmatrix}
>$$
