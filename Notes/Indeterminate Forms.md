> [!Abstract] Definition
> An **indeterminate form** arises from problems involving [[limits]]. 
> * The value of the **indeterminate form** depends on the specific **functions** involved, and is therefore **unknown**.

The **indeterminate forms** are:
* $\frac{0}{0}$
* $\frac{\infty}{\infty}$
* $0\cdot\infty$
* $\infty-\infty$
* $1^\infty$
* $0^0$
* $\infty^0$

> [!Note]
> $\frac{\infty}{0}$ is actually not **indeterminate**, since a massive number divided by a very small number tends to $\infty$.
