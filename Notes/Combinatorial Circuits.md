**Combinatorial circuits** are [[Logic Gates|logic circuits]] in which variables on the RHS of the [[Boolean Algebra|Boolean equation]] never appear on the LHS.
* This means there are never **cycles**.

**Combinatorial circuits** have two [[canonical forms]], [[minterms and maxterms]].
