There are two types of testing, **systematic testing** and **formal techniques**.

In **systematic testing**, the [[Synchronous Circuits|circuit]] is fed a large range of inputs, and the behaviour is observed.
* This is both done through simulation, and physical tests after manufacture.
* However, it is not unfeasible, to test every single possible input.

In **formal techniques**, the design is verified before manufacturing using advanced mathematical approaches.
* As CPUs grow, it becomes very challenging to reason about all possible behaviours, making this approach also limited.
